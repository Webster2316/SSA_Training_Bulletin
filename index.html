For Action

Members are invited to review the revised guidelines and submit comments to the Secretariat. The circular updates watch scheduling requirements and introduces new rest hour documentation standards. Please flag any operational concerns.

For Noting

All Ordinary Members

The Asian Shipowners' Association (ASA) held its 35th AGM in the Cook Islands, hosted by CISOA. ASA condemned attacks on civilian ships and seafarers in the Persian Gulf and called for emergency maritime safety corridors through international cooperation to protect freedom of navigation. Chairperson Eleanor Keukura Roi highlighted rising cyber threats (GPS spoofing, ransomware) and the need for enhanced resilience. On war risk insurance, ASA noted coverage gaps when hostile acts occur outside formally designated high‑risk areas. IMO Secretary‑General Arsenio Dominguez warned that without safe shipping, essential goods cannot be delivered globally. Mr Hitoshi Nagasawa (JSA) was appointed 36th ASA Chairperson, and Mr Jong Seug Park (KSA) as Vice‑Chairperson. The next AGM will be held in Hokkaido, Japan (2–4 June 2027).
